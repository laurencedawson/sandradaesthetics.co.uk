Wrinkle Relaxing Injections

Wrinkle relaxing injections are a simple, safe and effective way of reducing lines and wrinkles on the face caused by the underlying muscles used for expression. This non surgical treatment is the UK's number 1 requested treatment for facial lines and wrinkles.

How do wrinkles and lines occur?

Smiling, frowning, squinting all use facial muscles. The repeated use of these muscles leads to the formation of lines and wrinkles around the eyes ( crows feet) on the forehead and between the eyebrows (glabella). In addition the elasticity of the skin reduces over time, making the lines look deeper.

How does the treatment work?

A series of targeted tiny injections of the protein Botulinum Toxin are given in to the specific muscles that cause the lines . This protein reduces the activity of the muscle and relaxes the contraction of the muscle. This results in a reduction in the appearance of the lines as the skin smooths. Results can be first seen 2-­3 days after treatment with full effect at 14 days. The treatment last for 3­-4 months. With repeated treatment the effects may last longer.

Who can have wrinkle relaxing Injections?

Treatment would be avoided for people in the following groups:

  • Allergies to botulinum toxin product
  • History of Bells palsy
  • Pregnant or nursing mothers
  • Myasthenia Gravis or Lambert Eaton Syndrome
  • Heavily wrinkled skin from sun damage as the injections can not improve the skins elasticity
  • Taking aminoglycoside antibiotics
  • Blood clotting disorders or taking medications that can affect blood clotting

As part of the initial free consultation Sandra will discuss all aspects of the procedure with you and assess your suitability. A full medical history will be taken before treatment is given.

What are the side effects?

There may be slight discomfort from the needle entering the skin with a small chance of bruising at the injection site. Rare side effects in 1% of people can be lowering of the eyebrow or eyelid. This is a temporary effect and will return to normal.
