Dermal fillers

The dermal fillers Sandra uses are Volift, Vobella and the Juvederm Ultra range. Small amounts of the dermal fillers are injected into the skin or lips. Lines are softened, volume replaced giving a natural refreshed and rejuvenated appearance.

Depending on the product used, the results can last from 6 to 15 months. Modern dermal fillers consist of Hyaluronic Acid gel, a naturally occurring sugar. When injected into the skin they restore volume and hydration, this instantly replaces volume and smooths lines and wrinkles. The dermal filler also attracts water, helping keep the skin supple, hydrated and giving a more youthful appearance.

Where are they used?

Dermal fillers can be used in any area that needs volume restored or would benefit from skin hydration can benefit. The treatments Sandra offers are to the lips, nose to mouth lines, marionette lines, or fine lines.

What does the procedure involve?

Sandra will carefully evaluate your skin tone and facial appearance. The type and volume of gel used is determined by the depth and location of the treatment area. The skin is cleaned and anaesthetic cream is applied as necessary. The dermal fillers used also contain Lidocaine­ an anaesthetic to ease the procedure. The results are visible immediately.

After the treatment

Redness or swelling may initially occur but this will subside soon after treatment. Some mild bruising may occur. Cold compression will be offered. Some people return to work straight away.
