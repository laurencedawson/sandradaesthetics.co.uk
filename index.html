About Sandra

Sandra is a highly experienced Registered General Nurse who is also a qualified Nurse Independent Prescriber. With over 30 years of nursing experience Sandra has completed training in aesthetics allowing her to perform wrinkle relaxing injections and administer dermal fillers. She also continues to work in the NHS maintaining her professional education and requirements as indicated by the Nursing and Midwifery Council.

Sandra provides a free consultation before any treatment is given allowing time to consider your requirements, suitability for treatment and discuss the treatment before you make a decision. Any treatment would be at a follow up appointment.

Sandra offers a mobile service and is happy to see you in your own home in and around Peterborough providing a professional, discrete and flexible aesthetic treatments service.
